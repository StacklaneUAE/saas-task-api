Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

A focused toolset that gets out of your way.

⚡

Instant CRUD

Add, edit, complete and delete tasks with zero lag — every action hits a real REST API under the hood.

📊

Live progress

A real-time donut chart and counters show exactly how much of your day is already done.

🎉

Delightful motion

Confetti on completion, smooth reveals, and micro-interactions that make checking things off fun.

🔍

Smart filters

Flip between All, Active and Completed instantly to focus on what matters right now.

📱

Fully responsive

Looks sharp from a 4K monitor down to your phone, with a layout that adapts gracefully.

🪶

Featherweight

Pure Python standard library plus vanilla JS. No frameworks, no build step, runs anywhere.

Run the server

2

Open the app

Your browser opens to a clean board where you can capture tasks the moment they come to mind.

3

Check things off

Complete tasks and watch your progress climb — with a little celebration each time.
